📌 Common Questions

▶ Game Center leaderboard or achievements not updating?
Make sure you are signed into Game Center in iOS Settings. Also, try restarting the game or logging out and back into Game Center. Your progress is safe and will sync once the connection is restored.
▶ iCloud save not syncing across devices?
Verify that iCloud Drive is enabled for PixelDriftRally (Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Show All > PixelDriftRally). Also ensure you're using the same Apple ID on all devices.
▶ How do I delete my match data or reset progress?
You can request data deletion by emailing us. For Game Center reset, go to Game Center settings > "Reset Achievements". For iCloud saves, turn off sync in iCloud settings.
▶ Where can I find the Privacy Policy?
Our Privacy Policy is available at the link provided in the App Store listing. If you need a direct copy, please email us and we'll send it to you.

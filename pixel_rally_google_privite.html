Pixel Drift Rally – Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the game “Pixel Drift Rally” (referred to as “the Game”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Game. By installing or using the Game, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information we collect

The Game itself does not collect personally identifiable information such as your real name, email address, or phone number. However, we integrate third-party services that may collect certain information automatically.

1.1 Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display ads in the Game. AdMob may collect and process the following types of data:

  • Device information (such as device model, operating system version)
  • Advertising identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID)
  • Approximate location (not precise GPS)
  • App usage data related to ads (impressions, clicks, etc.)
  • Crash and performance data related to ad delivery

The collection and use of this data is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy, which you can review here:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

1.2 Game data storage (Firebase Firestore)

We use Firebase Firestore to store game-related data in the cloud, including but not limited to:

  • An anonymous player identifier (generated by Firebase or the Game)
  • Game progress (such as levels, coins, unlocked items)
  • Leaderboard data (such as scores, times, and related game stats)

We do not store your real name, email address, or other directly identifying personal information in Firestore.

For more information about Firebase privacy and security, please see:
https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

2. How we use the information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To display ads and support the monetization of the Game
  • To store and synchronize your game progress
  • To provide leaderboard features and rankings
  • To improve the stability, performance, and overall experience of the Game
  • To prevent cheating, abuse, or fraudulent activity

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties.

3. Third-party services

The Game uses third-party services that may collect information as described in their own privacy policies. These include:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Firebase Firestore (cloud data storage)

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these services to understand how they handle your data.

4. Children’s privacy

The Game is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If you believe that we have unintentionally collected personal information from a child, please contact us and we will promptly delete such data.

5. Data security

We rely on Firebase’s security features and industry-standard measures to help protect stored data, including:

  • Encrypted data transfer (HTTPS)
  • Firestore security rules to restrict unauthorized access
  • Use of anonymous identifiers instead of real-world identity data

However, no method of transmission or storage over the Internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your choices

You can stop all data collection by the Game and its services by uninstalling the Game from your device. If you would like us to delete game data associated with your anonymous identifier (such as leaderboard entries), you may contact us using the information below.
